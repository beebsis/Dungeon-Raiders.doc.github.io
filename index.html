Dungeon Raiders is a cooperative RPG game played via Twitch chat. Build your town step by step and unlock new features, upgrade existing ones and become the strongest town in Twitch! Simply choose your desired class and start playing, you will be able to build your character as you want as you level up. Gather with other players and defeat endless waves of enemies to get equipment and save the world!
