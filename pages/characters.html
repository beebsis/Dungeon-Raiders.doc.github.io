Characters

Warrior

Warriors have the highest defensive stats. The class is perfect to ensure protection of your town, and it is a reliable front line unit.

Capped stats
Attack Speed: 15/s
Dodge Chance: 50%

Stat Weights
Damage: * *
Resistance: * * * * *
Utility: * *

Slash

Tier I:

Deal physical damage to multiple enemies in front of you. Damage scales with damage [1] and your skill level.

Tier II:

Reduce hit enemies armor.

Fortify

Tier I:

Slightly heal yourself, temporarily increase your armor [4] and gain extra HP regeneration [7].

Tier II:

Increase your max HP for the rest of the combat.

Barbed Shield

Tier I:

Deal physical damage to multiple enemies in front of you. Damage scales with damage [1] and your skill level.

Tier II:

Reduce your attackers attack speed each time they get damaged by your ability

Rogue

Rogue have high offensive stats, and they also benefit from their high dodge capacity, which makes them a really powerful class for raiding.

Capped stats
Attack Speed: 20/s
Dodge Chance: 75%

Stat Weights
Damage: * * * * *
Resistance: * * * *
Utility: * *

Deep Cut

Tier I:

Deal a high amount of special damage to your current target. If it results in a kill, recover half of the fury cost. The damage scales with damage and your skill level.

Tier II:

Apply bleeding and if you execute the enemy with Deep Cut, recover more fury and gain a movement speed boost for a short period of time.

Dagger Burst

Tier I:

Increase your maximum attack speed and your attack speed for a few attacks. The attack speed buff and number of attacks is based on your skill level.

Tier II:

Increases your critical chance and critical chance cap and your daggers hit multiple enemies in front of you. Deals physical damage.

Smokoe Bomb

Tier I:

Instantly heal yourself for a small amount of HP. gain some HP/s and increase your maximum dodge change (+25%) and your dodge chance for a few seconds. The healing scales with with your maximum HP and skill level, and the duration scales with your skill level.

Tier II:

While the ability is active, a poisonous cloud surrounds you, constantly applying 1 poison stack to nearby enemies.

Farmer

Farmers are the best option if you like staying in town, as they earn extra resources from farming and mining. However, you can still have a big impact on raids too.

Capped stats
Attack Speed: 15/s
Dodge Chance: 80%

Stat Weights
Damage: * * *
Resistance: * * * *
Utility: * * * * *

Inner Strenght

Tier I:

Take out the strength that you carry inside, increasing all your base stats [1-9] for a few seconds. The duration and stat increase scale with your skill level.

Tier II:

Unleash a big amount of energy dealing massive special damage to nearby enemies.

Gluttony

Tier I:

Eat your favourite food, which instantly heals you.. The healing is based on your maximum HP and skill level.

Tier II:

If you or your ally overheal with this ability, deal part of the total excess heals to your target as special damage.

Fatigue

Tier I:

Hit your enemies with poisonous herbs, dealing a small amount of special damage to multiple enemies in front of you and reduce their attack speed for the rest of the fight. The damage dealt scales with your damage and skill level.

Tier II:

Enemies hit get their damage reduced.

Wizard

Wizards dominate the power of magic and are able to cast powerful spells, giving advantage to their allies on the battlefield. Their base attack damage is weak, but their abilities have high scaling.

Capped stats
Attack Speed: 10/s
Dodge Chance: 80%

Stat Weights
Damage: * * * *
Resistance: * *
Utility: * * * * *

Thunder Strike

Tier I:

Summon an electric cloud above your enemies that will strike nearby enemies with thunder. Duration of the cloud scales with skill level, and damage of the thunders scales with damage [1] and skill level.

Tier II:

If a thunder kills an enemy, small electric bolts will come out from its corpse, dealing damage over time to nearby enemies. This effect can retrigger with itself. Deals special damage.

Fire Shield

Tier I:

Cast a fire shield spell to your ally and yourself, increasing your damage [1], armor [4] and healing a small amount of HP [9]. Duration of the shield scales with skill level, all other effects scale with damage and skill level.

Tier II:

Fire shield grants damage reflection.

Polymorph

Tier I:

Transform your current target into a weak creature. While polymorphed, its armor and damage are reduced, their current skill gets canceled, and they can no longer generate fury. Duration scales with skill level.

Tier II:

UNKNOWN.

Ranger

Rangers provide consistent damage in raids. With a proper front line Rangers can be lethal, but they are weak when exposed to enemies by themself

Capped stats
Attack Speed: 20/s
Dodge Chance: 80%

Stat Weights
Damage: * * * * *
Resistance: * *
Dodge Chance: * * *

Precision

Tier I:

Focus on your enemy’s head. Increasing your critical chance and critical chance [8] and critical chance cap, securing your shot for a few attacks, having a 100% hit chance. Number of attacks and critical chance increase scale with skill level.

Tier II:

Increase your attack speed, attack speed cap and attack range while the ability is active.

Piercing Arrow

Tier I:

Your arrow will penetrate your target, dealing special damage and allowing you to hit multiple enemies per shot. The amount of damage is based on your skill level.

Tier II:

Increase the amount of enemies arrows can penetrate and apply bleeding to all enemies hit.

Poison Arrow

Tier I:

Shoot a poisonous arrow that will stick to your target, slowing it down and dealing poison damage [1] over time. The amount of slow scales with skill level, and the poison damage scales with damage [1] and skill level.

Tier II:

Increase the amount of poisonous arrows you can shoot with each ability cast
