Commands

Get started

In order to get started you'll have to make a character by typing !join class. You can pick between 5 classes, farmer, rogue, warrior, ranger and wizard. Each class has their pros and cons. We recommend trying each one out for a bit. You can read more about the different classes and their utility in the game on the Character section.

Command: ! join class

Each Character has 3 skills, you might want to change skill depending on the situation, composition of for testing. There are both defense, offense and utility abilities in this game. Use them according to the situation.

Relics

Relics can be obtained by defeating Dungeon Bosses and Underworld Guardians, as well as buying them in the Town Shop where they are on sale and they can be in chests. Relics require level 100+ to equip, and are linked to the account. You can upgrade their effects by enhancing them with the help from the Witch. After a first portal raid you can find the witch. In the chapter about the town there is more information about the witch
You gain relic slots at: lvl 100, level 500 & level 1000

Command: ! myrelics - Checks your relics

Command: ! setrelic# # - Sets a relic in the specified slot

List of relics:

1.) Undying Faith After receiving lethal damage, you are invulnerable for a few seconds, then you recover all your HP (1 time only). Effect: Invulnerable for 3s + 1s per level.
2.) Divine Grace Your basic attacks will execute demons when they are low on HP. (50% effect on Guardians). Effect: Execute when reaching 15% hp + 5% per level.
3.) Blood Transmutator Convert some of the exceeding healing you get into a shield. Effect: Maximum shield capacity equals 15% of your max HP + 5% per level
4.) Combat Knowledge Your ability fury cost increase is lower. Effect: Divide the base cost increase by 2 + level.
5.) Tree of Life Increase your HP/s depending on how much HP you are missing. Effect: 50% + 25% per level of your HP/s at 0% HP.
6.) Warlord Your special damage will benefit from part of your lifesteal. Effect: 15% of your lifesteal + 5% per level.
7.) Executioner Your critical strikes deal even more damage. Effect: 3x damage + 1x per level.
8.) Invisibility Cloak You can dodge critical strikes and mitigate a higher amount of damage plus reduce enemy fury gain when dodging. Effect: Divide the damage received when dodging by 2 + level.
9.) Kenji’s staff When casting your ability, deal special damage based on your fury and damage to nearby enemies. Effect: Damage dealt is your Damage/2000 * Fury/hit * (100 + level*10). Range is 200 + 10 per level.
10.) Claws of Sim While you are fighting, enemy deaths increase your damage for 6s. Effect: Damage increased by 0.005% + 0.0025% of your base Damage per level.
11.) Shield of Sim While you are fighting, enemy deaths increase your HP for 6s. Effect: HP increased by 0.005% + 0.0025% of your base Max HP per level. ! train
12.) Armor Oxidizer Your basic attacks will reduce your target's armor. Effect: -0.002 + 0.001 armor per level.
13.) Guardian’s Covenant Redirect part of the damage your allies take to you as special damage (does not have effect on redirected damage). Effect: Divide the damage taken by 1.10 + 0.05 per level.
14.) Unstable Matter Your special damage has a 50% chance of dealing increased damage. Effect: 50% + 25% extra damage per level.

Gold corn

Note that there are 2 types of damage: physical damage and special damage: Physical damage gets reduced by the target’s armor, and can also result in a dodge or a critical hit, as well as benefiting from lifesteal. Special damage cannot critically strike, be dodged, or apply lifesteal, but it will ignore 50% of the target’s armor. Additionally, there are status effects: bleed and poison: Bleed deals 10% of the target’s max HP over 3 seconds and reduces its healing by 50%, it can not stack with other bleed fonts, in case that happens, the 3 second timer will reset but no damage will be added. Bleed damage does not get reduced by armor. Poison also deals damage over time, but unlike bleeding it can stack multiple times, not resetting the duration but adding the damage. Poison will also slow its target, and will ignore 50% of the target’s armor. Each stack lasts 5s. Status effects do not count as special damage.

Auras

Auras are an extra stat bonus that will increase without limit as you progress. They are obtained from Guardians in the Underworld, and everyone that enters the portal will get them, no matter if they survived the floor where it got dropped or not. You can project your Aura at level 200+ and you can only have 1 active Aura at a time. You need some Aura before you can use it, so join a Portal run, but for that you need to be at level 200 or higher. Not every aura will become available during a Portal raid, so you may need to join more portal raids before you can activate the one you want to use.

Command: ! setaura # - Set your aura

Requires: Hero+ status

Command: ! setraidaura # - Set an aura for raiding/guarding

Command: ! setmineaura # - Set an aura for mining

Command: ! settrainaura # - Sets an aura for traning

List of auras:

1.) Vitality Increases Maximum hitpoints and healing
2.) Wisdom Increases experiance gain
3.) Braveness Damange and critical chance increase
4.) Abundance Gold & farming rate increase

Buildings

Upgrading a building will give you benefits on that particular building and also increase your town maximum HP. A few buildings have two numbers next to them. The (x/y) indicates how many players are using the building (x) and the (y) the maximum number that that can be present at the same time

Command: ! upg (building name) - Upgrades the building

Command: ! voteyes - Votes yes for building upgrade

Command: ! voteno - Votes no for building upgrade
